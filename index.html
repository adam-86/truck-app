Instructions

Step 1:

Use the arrows to navigate through the slides and pick your favorite truck.

Step 2:

Click the pointer icon and you will be prompted to upload an image from your device.

Step 3:

After selecting an image, you will be presented with a crop box. Line your image up the best you can and click the accept button.

Step 4:

If you would like to save a copy, click the download button positioned below your image.
